# NetSnap Terms & Conditions (MVP)

Last updated: 2026-01-22

## 1) What NetSnap Is
NetSnap is a local-first payout analyzer that helps summarize uploaded payout/statement files and produce exports and insights.

## 2) Your Responsibility for Uploaded Data
You are responsible for the files you upload.

Do not upload:
- Social Security numbers, full bank account numbers, or government IDs
- Medical records or private health information
- Any data you do not have the right to use

## 3) No Financial, Tax, or Legal Advice
NetSnap provides summaries and reporting tools. It is not financial, tax, or legal advice. You are responsible for decisions made using the output.

## 4) Accuracy and Limitations (MVP)
NetSnap attempts to categorize transactions based on patterns and platform statement formats. Results may be incomplete or inaccurate if:
- a platform changes its export format
- files are edited, partial, or duplicated
- unsupported file types are uploaded

You should validate results against your original statements when accuracy matters.

## 5) Support and Bug Reports
If you submit a bug report or optional redacted sample bundle, you consent to NetSnap receiving the submitted information for troubleshooting and product improvement.

## 6) Acceptable Use

- attempt to reverse engineer or exploit the product
- interfere with service reliability

## 7) Availability
NetSnap may be updated, changed, or temporarily unavailable. Features may be added or removed in the MVP phase.
